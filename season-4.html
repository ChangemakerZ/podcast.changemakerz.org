Let's go for GenZers to rise! Season 4

It's official, GenZers to rise! Podcast Season 4 is coming out at 29th of June 2022. We are coming with a brand new idenity for our show, with great guests topics and an amazing new app for you to be engaged with our content and Thred Media.

“You just need one person to listen, get your message, and pass it on to someone else. And you’ve doubled your audience.”

What's included and what have changed ?

In season one, we interviewed guests such as Thred Media founder Jenk Oz, youtube star Gawx, and Rayze Consulting founders RaEyan and Sahil.

In season two, we expanded the topics by talking with guests like Jeena Chong, the CEO of a clean, vegan skincare brand, and conversationalist Sophie Beren.

In season three we had the opportunity to be expanded on art topics like the Macrame art, music production, and we discovered new working and orginization ideas like the digital nomad phenomenon. Last but not least, we touched on some important issues including organ donation and the production of cleaning products from pine needles.

Over the past three seasons, we've interviewed over 32 guests, and season four is going to be even better. We have a new cover for our show which is more personalised.

In addition to that, we have already booked a wide variety of guests that they are leaders on their field. We are also celebrating the transition period from our previous editing process to the new one which is now based on audio direction. This means that we are incorporating feelings in our episodes via sounds and music provided from our independent artists.

Our mantra

We started this podcast in January 2021 as a response to the COVID-19 quarantine. It was our interactive way to get connected with the world. Nowadays, we are dedicated to create more intresting content that can help our listeners to believe on themselves and start their own projects. We think that this is the passion of every creator. Are you ready to change the world ?

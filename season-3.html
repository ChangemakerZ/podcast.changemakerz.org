Let's go for GenZers to rise! Season 3

It's official, GenZers to rise! Podcast Season 3 is coming out at 23th of January 2022. The first time that we will have real sponshors onto the show thanks to all our international audience.

“How has podcasting changed things? A lot of people ask me if I feel I should be more famous.”

What's included and what have changed ?

In season one, we interviewed guests such as Thred Media founder Jenk Oz, youtube star Gawx, and Rayze Consulting founders RaEyan and Sahil.

In season two, we expanded the topics by talking with guests like Jeena Chong, the CEO of a clean, vegan skincare brand, and conversationalist Sophie Beren. Over the past two seasons, we've interviewed over 20 guests, and season three is going to be even bigger and better.

We have many goals that we're excited to share with you guys. First off, we will continue to work with a wide range of guests to bring new and interesting discussion topics to the podcast. We can't wait for more conversation-based episodes like the COP26 discussion with Ghislaine and JP.

In addition to new guests, we want to incorporate your voices into the podcast (with voice messages in every episode ending)! None of this would be possible without you guys, so we will start adding fan voice messages at the end of the episodes. We will also create an open discussion community (like discord) through our brand new ChangemakerZ app, available both on iOS and Android.

Finally, GenZers to Rise! will be available on YouTube! Yep, you will now be able to find us on Youtube as well as the other platforms we currently have.

GenZers to rise! mode activated

The changemakerz.org mission

ChangeMakerz continues to grow with over 10k visits on our main website Changemakerz.org. There we house more content specifically for Gen Z by Gen Z. Including more interviews with entrepreneurs and some of our favorite articles and Youtube content. You can also find our podcast with descriptions of each episode, so you can find one that interests you! Always remember that “good promotion is the key. In combination with the right software, everything is possible.”

Our NEW mantra

We started this podcast in January 2021 as a response to the COVID-19 quarantine. It was our interactive way to get connected with the world. Nowadays, we are dedicated to create more intresting content that can help our listeners to believe on themselves and start their own projects. We think that this is the passion of every creator. Are you ready to change the world ?

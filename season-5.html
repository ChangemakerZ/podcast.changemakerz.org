Let's go for GenZers to rise! Season 5

It's official, GenZers to rise! Podcast Season 5 is coming out at 23rd of February 2023. We are coming with a brand new lineup of guests, with changes to our hosts and an amazing upgraded version of the app for you to be engaged with our content and Thred Media.

What to expect from season 5 ?

Hey Changemakers! Welcome to Season 5 of GenzerstoRise, where we connect with inspiring leaders and innovators from around the world. In this season, we'll be featuring a diverse lineup of guests, including John Mendez, Mateusz Piesak portfolio wildlife photographer of the year, Efrosini Dimitropoulou, Vasundhara - founder of inspire dose, Hasrha Ravidran - founder of Ascendance and more.

In previous seasons, we've explored a wide range of topics, from the future of technology and entrepreneurship to the power of storytelling and creativity. Our guests have shared their insights, experiences, and wisdom, and we're excited to continue that journey in this new season.

Join us as we dive into the latest trends and issues facing our world today, and learn from some of the most dynamic and impactful voices in the fields of business, politics, technology, and more. It's going to be an exciting season, so be sure to tune in and don't miss a single episode. Remember if they can do it, so can you!

Our mantra

We started this podcast in January 2021 as a response to the COVID-19 quarantine. It was our interactive way to get connected with the world. Nowadays, we are dedicated to create more intresting content that can help our listeners to believe on themselves and start their own projects. We think that this is the passion of every creator.

Spotify Wrapped 2023 for GenZers to rise! Podcast

Another year is coming to its end and Spotify Wrapped 2023 is here! This is the third Spotify Wrapped for the GenZers to rise! Podcast. We were waiting with such an excitement this year report and the results left us speechless.

This year we produced 323 minutes of content. This is a lot of content! For one more year, the top episode was "Turning a passion into a successful career with Gawx". You nailed it since this episode had 999% more listens than an average episode.

Only on Spotify, our listens came from 36 different countries. This year top 5 are India, France, Netherlands, Poland and Brazil.

Nothing would be possible without our super engaged listeners. You are devotees which stands for "When your fans love a podcast, they really love it. They're quick to support new releases and play their favorite episodes over and over". We achieved a wonderful rating of 4.6 out of 5 this year.

Our podcast saw gains this year - 17% more followers. We really had a moment! We are a top 10 podcast for 124 fans, a top 5 podcast for 96 fans and #1 podcast for 35 fans! Thanks every one for your support. See you next year...
